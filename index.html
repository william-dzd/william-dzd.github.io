Biography

I received my Ph.D degree in the Department of Systems Engineering and Engineering Management from the Chinese University of Hong Kong, supervised by Prof. Shiqian Ma and Prof. Anthony Man-Cho So. Before joining CUHK, I obtained my bachelor degree in Statistics from Nankai University.

My research interests mainly focus on convex/nonconvex constrained optimization problems that arise from machine learning, statistics, and signal processing.
