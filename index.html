Zengde Deng

I am an algorithm engineer at Cainiao AI, Alibaba Group, Hangzhou, where I work on machine learning, operations research, logistics, supply chain, etc. I received my Ph.D degree in the Department of Systems Engineering and Engineering Management from the Chinese University of Hong Kong, supervised by Prof. Shiqian Ma and Prof. Anthony Man-Cho So. Before joining CUHK, I obtained my bachelor degree in Statistics from Nankai University.
